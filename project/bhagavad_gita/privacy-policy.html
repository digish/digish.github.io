Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 19, 2025

We respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the Bhagavad Gita - Search within app handles your data.

Summary: We do not collect, store, or share any personal data.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Our application is designed to function completely offline (after initial download) for its core features. We do not require you to register, sign in, or provide any personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

2. Data Storage

All user-generated content, including:

  • Bookmarks
  • Custom Lists
  • Reading History
  • Settings and Preferences

...is stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences and a local definition database (SQLite). This data stays on your phone and is not transmitted to us or any third party.

3. Permissions

The app may request the following permissions for specific functionalities:

  • Notifications: To send you the "Daily Shloka". This is handled locally on your device.
  • Audio/Media: To play Shloka recitations.

4. Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party analytics services (like Google Analytics or Firebase) that track user behavior.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by an updated "Revised" date.
